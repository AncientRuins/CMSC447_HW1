About Me

Hi, I am Nishwanth Gudibandla. I was born in India in 2003 and moved here when I was 2.5 years old. At first, I lived in Connecticut in a small town called Danbury. In second grade, I moved here to Howard County, Maryland, where I have lived for the past 12 years.

Currently, I am a sophmore undergrad at University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC). I am studying for a Physics and Computer Science Dual Degree. At the moment, I am part of two clubs oncampus: League of Legends Club and the UMBC Esports Club. I am the treasurer for the League of Legends Club and am, in essence, a board member for the Esports club. For the fall semester that just passed, I was also a substitute for the League club's second esports squad.

From the age of 6, I played soccer. At first it was recreationally, but eventually I played with a travel team in local tournaments. When I was 16, I stopped playing so that I could focus more on my education. Despite this, I still watch professional soccer games when I get the chance. My favorite club is Manchester City and my favorite player is Messi.

I enjoy watching most sports, as they are quite entertaining. Whenever they are available for viewing, I tend to watch them. If there are multiple different sports available at the same time, I have a hierarchy of interest. In order from greatest preference down, it would be something like soccer, american football, ice hockey, baseball, basketball, golf, tennis, and then everything else.

I have two primary hobbies: reading books and playing video games. Whenever, I am not busy with schoolwork, I am doing one of these activities. Nowadays, it is more often then not video games rather than reading.

As far as games go, I enjoy RTS, MOBAs, FPS, and MMO/RPG games the most. For RTS games, I mostly play Paradox games like Hearts of Iron IV and Stellaris. For MOBAs, I play League of Legends and, on rare occasions, Dota. For FPS games, I play a lot of Valorant and Counterstrike. In the past, I played some Overwatch, but a lot of the gameplay decisions by Blizzard turned me away. Finally, for RPG games, I play Genshin, Minecraft, and Path of Exile. I used to play some MMO games, but the time investment just became too large. However, I still do log back in and play for a bit when I have the motivation to.

Fun Facts

This section contains some random fun facts.

1. The world's largest desert is the Antarctic Desert [2]. By definition, "a desert is a barren area of landscape where little precipitation occurs" [3]. The entire continent of Antarctica receives only 10 mm of precipitation every year, which classifies it as a desert. The massive ice sheets we see on the continent were built over the course of several hundred millennia.
